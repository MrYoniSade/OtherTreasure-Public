Child Safety Standards

OtherTreasure is a hyperlocal marketplace for giving away and finding free items. It is intended for users aged 18 and older. We are committed to protecting children from sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and fully comply with Google Play's child safety standards policy.

Age Restriction

OtherTreasure is not directed at children. Registration requires an authenticated account and the app is intended for adults only. Users who are found to be under the age of 18 will have their accounts removed.

Prohibited Content

OtherTreasure has a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any content that sexualises, exploits, or endangers minors. Any such content is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account termination and reporting to the relevant authorities.

Reporting Concerns

Users can report child safety concerns directly to us via the in-app "Report a Problem" feature or by emailing us at:

othertreasuremarketing@gmail.com

All reports are reviewed promptly. Where required by law, we will report to regional and national authorities including law enforcement.

Compliance

OtherTreasure complies with all relevant child safety laws and regulations, including obligations to report CSAM to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) and equivalent authorities in applicable jurisdictions.
