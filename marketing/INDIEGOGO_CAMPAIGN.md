## Campaign description (ready to paste into Indiegogo)

---

### The problem

Every day, millions of useful items — furniture, clothes, toys, appliances, books — get thrown in the bin.

Not because they're broken. Because throwing away is easier than giving away.

Existing platforms (Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist) are cluttered with paid listings, scam offers, and negotiation. Free stuff drowns in the noise.

---

### Our solution

**OtherTreasure** is a hyperlocal map that shows only free items available right now near you.

- Open the app → see what your neighbours are giving away
- Tap a marker → see a photo, condition, and pickup details
- One tap to navigate → pick it up

**Posting is just as fast:**
- Take a photo
- Add a title and category
- Your item appears on the map in seconds

No accounts to verify. No negotiations. No price. Just free.

---

### Why this matters

- The average household throws away **£140 worth of reusable items per year** (WRAP UK)
- Landfill fees and carbon cost of manufacturing replacements are hidden from individuals
- Local community apps create trust networks that reduce waste at the neighbourhood level

OtherTreasure turns "I should throw this out" into "someone near me could use this."

---

### What we've built so far

✅ Live map with item markers, radius filter, category filter  
✅ Add item in under 60 seconds (photo, title, condition, floor, elevator, availability)  
✅ Mark as taken — item disappears from map  
✅ Google, Facebook, and Apple sign-in  
✅ English, Hebrew, Russian — RTL support  
✅ "Navigate to item" via Google Maps / Waze  
✅ Automatic item expiry (items don't linger forever)  

The core app is **working and tested**. We need your support to launch it properly.

### Risks and challenges

We are transparent about where we are:

- The core app is built and tested. The main risk is **user adoption** — real people need to start posting items in the same area for the network effect to kick in. Our Facebook seeding strategy (see our blog) directly addresses this.
- **App store approval**: we have completed the technical requirements for both stores. There is always risk of rejection due to policy changes, but we have documented compliance with all guidelines.
- **Firebase cost scaling**: the app uses Firebase (pay-as-you-go). We have built-in budget alerts and a conservative cost model. At 1,000 MAU our estimated monthly Firebase spend is < $10.
