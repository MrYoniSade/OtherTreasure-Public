# Marketing Plan — OtherTreasure

> Goal: grow from 0 to 10,000+ users using zero-budget organic channels first, then amplify with a crowdfunding campaign.
> Core promise: **Give it away instead of throwing it away.** Free for givers. Free for takers. Good for the city.

> Experimental campaign line to test in selected channels: **Save our planet and find a fortune.** Use carefully as a hook for entrepreneurial or tech audiences, not as the default consumer-facing promise.

---

## Positioning statement

OtherTreasure is the **hyperlocal free-stuff map** — open the app, see what your neighbours are giving away today, grab it before it goes to landfill.

**Key differentiators vs. Facebook Marketplace / Craigslist / eBay:**
- 100% free — no selling, no price negotiation, no scam listings
- Map-first UX — see items physically near you, right now
- One-tap pickup routing (Google Maps / Waze)
- Zero friction to post — photo, title, done in under 60 seconds

## Campaign lines to test

- **Default public line:** Give it away instead of throwing it away.
- **Map-first line:** The free-stuff map for your city.
- **Founder / builder audience line:** Save our planet and find a fortune.

## Map product inspiration

- Use LiveUAmap as a reference for how a map product can make money from visibility, urgency, and local context.
- Apply the pattern to OtherTreasure with clearly labeled sponsored pins, partner service zones, neighborhood impact views, and municipal reporting.
- Keep the map readable; monetization cannot make discovery worse than the free product.

## Phase 1 — Launch (M1, $0 budget)

**Goal:** first 50–100 real users in 1 city/neighbourhood. Validate the core loop.

### 1.1 Facebook community seeding

See [FACEBOOK_PLAYBOOK.md](FACEBOOK_PLAYBOOK.md) for full script + post templates.

| Action | Effort | Cost |
|--------|--------|------|
| Join 10–20 local neighbourhood Facebook groups | 1 hour | Free |
| Post photo of a real item you're giving away + mention the app | 15 min/post | Free |
| Reply to every "free stuff" thread with a friendly mention | 10 min/day | Free |
| Create a dedicated "OtherTreasure — [City Name]" Facebook group | 30 min | Free |
| Pin a "how it works" post with screenshots | 30 min | Free |

**Target groups to join (search on Facebook):**
- "Free stuff [your city]"
- "Buy nothing [your city/neighbourhood]"
- "Freecycle [your city]"
- "[City name] neighbourhood group"
- "[City name] parents / families group"
- "Zero waste [your city]"
- "Sustainability [your city]"

### 1.2 WhatsApp & Telegram seeding

- Post in family / friends / neighbours groups with a real item to give away
- Share a short screen-recording (10 seconds) showing how easy posting is
- Ask 5 friends to post their first item — offer to help them do it
- Test the entrepreneurial hook in founder/tech circles only; keep neighborhood channels focused on usefulness and trust

### 1.3 Local Reddit & Discord

- Post in r/[yourcity] with a genuine story: "I made this app to stop throwing things away — try posting your first free item"
- Engage organically, never spam

### 1.4 App Store / Play Store optimisation (ASO)

- Title: **OtherTreasure — Free Stuff Near You**
- Subtitle: Give away instead of throwing away
- Keywords: free stuff, free items, give away, freecycle, local map, reuse
- Screenshots: show the map with real item markers, item card, and "navigate to" button
- Short description (Play Store): "See free items on a live map. Post yours in 60 seconds. No selling, no scams."

### 1.5 Content seeds (create real activity fast)

Before public launch, you must seed the map:
1. Post 5–10 real items yourself from different spots in the city
2. Ask 5 friends/family to post real items (offer to photograph them yourself)
3. Goal: when a new user opens the app there are at least 10 visible items

## Phase 2 — Early traction (M2, ~$0–$50/month)

**Goal:** 100+ MAU, first organic word-of-mouth loop running.

| Action | Est. cost |
|--------|-----------|
| Ko-fi donation page live | Free |
| Basic website with landing page, sponsor lead form, municipal pitch page, and SEO posts | Domain + hosting only |
| "Support the app" banner shown after 3rd item claim | Free |
| First 3–5 pilot sponsors (local secondhand shops, movers) — DM them directly | Free |
| Produce 3 short Reels / TikToks showing "I found this for free" | Free |
| Submit app to "best free apps" lists and ProductHunt | Free |
| Collect 10 user testimonials — screenshot their reviews/messages | Free |
| Indiegogo campaign launch (see [INDIEGOGO_CAMPAIGN.md](INDIEGOGO_CAMPAIGN.md)) | ~$0 upfront |

### Website role in growth

- Capture SEO traffic for city-specific "free stuff map" searches.
- Convert sponsor and municipal leads without depending on app-store traffic.
- Host partnership pages, impact proof, and media assets.
- Reduce dependence on mobile subscription revenue that is subject to store fees.
