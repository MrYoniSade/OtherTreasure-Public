# Facebook Community Playbook — OtherTreasure

> Step-by-step guide to growing OtherTreasure using Facebook groups — **zero ad spend**.

---

## Strategy overview

Facebook local groups are the fastest zero-cost channel because:
- Millions of people already trade / give away items in neighbourhood groups
- Posts with a photo of a **real physical item** get organic reach without being flagged as spam
- We are genuinely adding value — not selling anything

**Core rule:** Always lead with a real item, not with "I made an app". People click because they want the item.

## Step 2 — First post (the item-first approach)

Never announce "I made an app" as your opening line. Post a real item. Mention the app as a footnote.

### Post template A — giving away an item

```
📦 Free to collect — [ITEM NAME], [NEIGHBOURHOOD/CITY]

[SHORT HONEST DESCRIPTION — 1-2 sentences, mention condition]

First to message me gets it 🙂

I've started using OtherTreasure app to post free items — 
it pins everything on a map so neighbours can see what's 
available right now. This item is live there too if you 
want to find me on the map:
👉 [App store link]

Pickup: [address hint e.g. "near the central train station"]
```

### Post template B — after someone claims an item (follow-up)

```
Claimed! 🎉 Thanks [first name], the [item] found a new home.

For anyone who missed it — I post free items regularly on the 
OtherTreasure app. You can browse what's available near you 
on a live map:
👉 [App store link]
```

### Post template C — joining a "free stuff" thread started by someone else

When you see someone posting a free item, comment:

```
Great that you're passing it on! I posted something similar 
last week on OtherTreasure — it puts free items on a map 
so locals can find them easily. Worth a look if you haven't 
tried it 👉 [link]
```

## Step 3 — Create a dedicated OtherTreasure group

Create: **"OtherTreasure — [City Name] Free Stuff Map"**

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Privacy | Public |
| Category | Buy and Sell / Community |
| Description | See box below |
| Cover photo | App screenshot showing the map with item markers |

**Group description (copy-paste ready):**
```
Welcome to OtherTreasure [City] 🗺️

This group is for people who want to give away useful items 
instead of throwing them away — and for people who want to 
find free stuff near them.

How it works:
✅ Post what you're giving away (photo + neighbourhood)
✅ Browse the OtherTreasure app to see items on a live map
✅ Message the giver and arrange a free pickup
✅ Items disappear from the map once taken

App: [App store link]

Rules:
- Free items only — no selling, trading, or paid services
- Include a photo and your general neighbourhood
- Mark your item as "taken" once it's gone
- Be respectful — this is a community of neighbours

Questions? DM the admin 👋
```

**Pinned posts to create on day 1:**
1. "How the app works" — 3 screenshots + bullet points
2. A real item you're giving away yourself
3. "Intro — who are we" post

## Step 4 — Daily 15-minute routine (first 30 days)

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Mon | Post 1 item in your city's largest neighbourhood group |
| Tue | Reply to 3 free-stuff threads in other groups |
| Wed | Post 1 item in a different group (parents / sustainability) |
| Thu | Share a "just claimed!" follow-up story in the dedicated group |
| Fri | DM 3 people who posted items this week — invite them to try the app |
| Sat | Post a "what's available this weekend" roundup in the dedicated group |
| Sun | Review comments, respond to all DMs |

---

## Step 5 — Group growth tactics (zero cost)

- **Invite friends** who live in the area — target 20 seed members in week 1
- **Cross-post** items between your group and other local groups (always mention the app)
- **Tag local eco-accounts** in your posts if they're public pages
- **Run a give-away challenge**: "Who gave away the most items this month?" — screenshot the leaderboard from the app
- **Celebrate milestones**: post "We just hit 50 members!" or "100 items given away in [city]!"

---

## Step 6 — What NOT to do (avoid being banned from groups)

- Do not post the same promotional text word-for-word in multiple groups on the same day
- Do not post without a real item — pure app promotion posts get removed
- Do not mass-add people to your dedicated group without their knowledge
- Do not respond to every comment with a link — be a real community member first
- Do not use automated posting tools that violate Facebook's terms of service
