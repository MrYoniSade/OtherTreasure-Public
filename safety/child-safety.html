1. Platform Scope

OtherTreasure is a local platform for giving away and finding free items. The app is designed for adults and is not directed to children.

2. Age Restriction

The minimum age to use OtherTreasure is 18. Accounts identified as minors may be suspended or removed.

3. Zero-Tolerance Policy

We have a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation material (CSAM/CSAE), or any content that endangers minors.

  • Such content is strictly prohibited.
  • Associated accounts are removed immediately.
  • Where legally required, we report to the relevant authorities.

4. Reporting and Moderation

Users can report concerning content or behavior from the app or by email:

othertreasuremarketing@gmail.com

Reports are reviewed promptly and we take moderation actions as needed.

5. Legal Compliance

We comply with applicable child protection laws and cooperate with relevant authorities and organizations when required.
